Overview

Purpose

Chicago's MoA is dedicated to collecting, preserving, and exhibiting exceptional works of art, spanning various cultures and time periods, with the aim of inspiring and enriching the lives of our visitors. We are committed to fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of the visual arts, providing educational opportunities, and serving as a cultural hub for our community. Through our diverse collections, engaging exhibitions, and educational programs, we aspire to ignite curiosity, spark creativity, and promote a shared sense of cultural heritage.

Audience

Chicago's MoA seeks to engage a diverse and inclusive audience, including: students, educators, families, and our local community. We are committed to serving as a valuable resource for students and educators at all levels, from K-12 to higher education, by providing educational programs, resources, and curriculum support. We aim to be a family-friendly institution, offering a range of programs and exhibitions suitable for visitors of all ages, fostering a love for art among the younger generation. And we are dedicated to being a cultural center and a point of pride for our local community, offering events, exhibitions, and initiatives that reflect the interests and values of the area.
